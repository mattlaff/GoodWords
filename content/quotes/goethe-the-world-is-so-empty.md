**"The world is so empty if one thinks only of mountains, rivers and cities; but to know someone here and there who thinks and feels with us, and though distant, is close to us in spirit — this makes the earth for us an inhabited garden."** _~Johann Wolfgang von Goethe_

---

Probably my favorite quote ever.

And as an elder millennial who grew up both online and off, I often think of this quote from a different angle: "_The internet is so empty if one thinks only of websites, search engines, and stores; but to know someone here and there here and there who thinks and feels with us, and though remote, is close to us in spirit — this makes the internet for us an inhabited garden._"

There is no meaning apart from **_we_**.
