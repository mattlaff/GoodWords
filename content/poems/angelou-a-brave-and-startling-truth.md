We, this people, on a small and lonely planet
Traveling through casual space
Past aloof stars, across the way of indifferent suns
To a destination where all signs tell us
It is possible and imperative that we learn
A brave and startling truth
[...]
When we come to it
We, this people, on this wayward, floating body
Created on this earth, of this earth
Have the power to fashion for this earth
A climate where every man and every woman
Can live freely without sanctimonious piety
Without crippling fear

([full poem](https://www.themarginalian.org/2018/05/09/a-brave-and-startling-truth-maya-angelou/))

---

The first poem I typed out when I started this practice, perhaps because in times that overwhelm it can be helpful to remember that we are not above the rest of creation but a small and simple part of it.
