Sometimes the mountain
is hidden from me in veils
of cloud, sometimes
I am hidden from the mountain
in veils of inattention

([full poem](https://web.archive.org/web/20260706184758/https://artsinmedicine.ufhealth.org/files/2013/06/Witness.pdf))

---

On attention, by far our most precious resource, and the mysteries it can reveal if we let it. I find returning to this poem regularly serves me well, reminds me of the importance of intention.

I don't know how she does it, but Denise Levertov speaks to me like very few poets do, and I didn't discover her work until I was nearly 40. Suffice to say, that might be when that the expression "better late than never" rang most true for me.
