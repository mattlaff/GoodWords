If you don't know the kind of person I am
and I don't know the kind of person you are
a pattern that others made may prevail in the world
and following the wrong god home we may miss our star.

[...]

For it is important that awake people be awake,
or a breaking line may discourage them back to sleep;
the signals we give — yes or no, or maybe —
should be clear: the darkness around us is deep.

([full poem](https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poems/58264/a-ritual-to-read-to-each-other))

---

I found a printed-out copy of this poem among some of my grandmother's things after she passed, and every time I return to this poem, I'm reminded that she recognized the importance of honest, intentional communication between two people and, in addition to the many other wonderful things I could say about her, that she did her best to be honest and open with the rest of us. Grandma knew what mattered, and I try to remind myself sometimes too.
